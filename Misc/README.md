# Claude Cowork Research & Analysis Demo Data

## Overview
This collection of datasets is designed to demonstrate the research and analysis capabilities of Claude Cowork. Each dataset represents realistic business scenarios where AI-powered analysis can provide actionable insights.

---

## Dataset Descriptions

### 1. Quarterly Sales Data (`quarterly_sales_data.csv`)
**Use Case:** Sales Performance Analysis & Trend Identification

**Key Columns:**
- Date, Region, Product Category, Product Name
- Sales metrics: Units Sold, Revenue, Cost, Profit
- Sales Rep, Customer Segment

**Analysis Opportunities:**
- Identify top-performing products, regions, and sales representatives
- Calculate profit margins and ROI by product category
- Analyze seasonal trends and customer segment preferences
- Compare regional performance and identify growth opportunities
- Detect underperforming products that may need marketing support

**Sample Questions to Ask Claude Cowork:**
- "Which product category generates the highest profit margin?"
- "Compare sales performance across regions and identify the fastest-growing market"
- "Create a report showing top 5 products by revenue with trend analysis"
- "Analyze Sarah Johnson's sales performance compared to team average"

---

### 2. Market Research Survey (`market_research_survey.csv`)
**Use Case:** Customer Satisfaction Analysis & Product Development Insights

**Key Columns:**
- Demographics: Age Group, Gender, Country, Industry
- Product metrics: Satisfaction Score, NPS, Feature Ratings
- Qualitative feedback: Pain Points, Feature Requests
- Business metrics: Company Size, Monthly Budget, Likelihood to Renew

**Analysis Opportunities:**
- Calculate Net Promoter Score (NPS) by product and segment
- Identify common pain points and feature requests
- Segment analysis by industry, company size, and geography
- Correlation between satisfaction scores and renewal likelihood
- Budget analysis to inform pricing strategy

**Sample Questions to Ask Claude Cowork:**
- "What are the top 3 feature requests across all products?"
- "Calculate average NPS by company size and identify improvement areas"
- "Which customer segments show highest satisfaction but lowest renewal rates?"
- "Create a summary of pain points for the Security Suite product"

---

### 3. Competitive Analysis (`competitive_analysis.csv`)
**Use Case:** Market Positioning & Competitive Intelligence

**Key Columns:**
- Company information: Name, Market Share, Headquarters
- Product metrics: Ratings, Feature Scores, Performance Scores
- Business metrics: User Base, Revenue, Growth Rate, Retention
- Feature comparison: Integration Options, Mobile App, API, Free Trial

**Analysis Opportunities:**
- Benchmark against competitors across multiple dimensions
- Identify market gaps and differentiation opportunities
- Analyze pricing tier strategies and market positioning
- Compare feature completeness and user satisfaction
- Understand competitive strengths and weaknesses

**Sample Questions to Ask Claude Cowork:**
- "Compare our CoWork Suite to top 3 competitors on key metrics"
- "Which competitors have the highest customer retention and why?"
- "Identify market gaps where we could differentiate our product"
- "Create a competitive positioning matrix based on price vs. features"

---

### 4. Website Analytics (`website_analytics.csv`)
**Use Case:** Marketing Performance & Conversion Optimization

**Key Columns:**
- Traffic metrics: Visitors, Page Views, Session Duration, Bounce Rate
- Conversion metrics: Sign Ups, Trial Starts, Conversions, Revenue
- Campaign data: Traffic Source, Campaign Name, Ad Spend, ROI
- Segmentation: Device Type, Country, Landing Page

**Analysis Opportunities:**
- Calculate ROI and conversion rates by traffic source and campaign
- Identify best-performing landing pages and campaigns
- Analyze user behavior patterns by device and geography
- Optimize marketing spend allocation
- Detect trends in organic vs. paid traffic performance

**Sample Questions to Ask Claude Cowork:**
- "Which marketing channel has the highest ROI?"
- "Compare conversion rates between mobile and desktop users"
- "Analyze bounce rates by landing page and recommend improvements"
- "Calculate cost per acquisition for each paid campaign"

---

### 5. Support Tickets (`support_tickets.csv`)
**Use Case:** Customer Support Optimization & Quality Improvement

**Key Columns:**
- Ticket details: Category, Priority, Status, Product
- Time metrics: Resolution Time, First Response Time
- Quality metrics: Customer Satisfaction, Escalation Rate
- Resource tracking: Agent Name, Resolution Type

**Analysis Opportunities:**
- Identify common issues and their root causes
- Analyze support team efficiency and response times
- Track customer satisfaction trends by product and issue type
- Predict tickets requiring escalation
- Optimize resource allocation based on ticket patterns

**Sample Questions to Ask Claude Cowork:**
- "What are the most common technical issues by product?"
- "Which support agents have the highest customer satisfaction scores?"
- "Calculate average resolution time by priority level and identify bottlenecks"
- "Analyze escalation patterns and recommend preventive measures"

### 6. Employee Performance (`employee_performance.csv`)
**Use Case:** HR Analytics & Talent Management

**Key Columns:**

**Analysis Opportunities:**

- Analyze training ROI and skill development trends
- Predict promotion readiness and succession planning
- Assess team performance by department and manager
- Correlate satisfaction scores with performance metrics

**Sample Questions to Ask Claude Cowork:**
- "Which departments have the highest average performance ratings?"
- "Identify employees eligible for promotion based on multiple criteria"
- "Analyze correlation between training hours and performance ratings"
- "Create a talent retention risk report based on satisfaction and tenure"
