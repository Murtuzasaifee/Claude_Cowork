6. Recommendations

  • 1
    Collections Priority
    Immediately chase the 43 overdue invoices. Focus first on the top-5 vendors by value (Velasquez-Ortega, Beck-Morton, Taylor-Odonnell, Newman Inc, Hooper Bauer & Brown) which collectively represent over 63% of total invoice exposure.
  • 2
    Reconciliation Triage
    Assign the 29 unmatched documents (total ~4,015 mixed-ccy) to AP/AR staff for vendor confirmation within 5 business days to prevent period-close delays.
  • 3
    Batch Payment Review
    Investigate the 21 many-to-one bank transactions. Ensure each bundled payment has appropriate remittance advice on file and the total matches the sum of linked document amounts.
  • 4
    VOID Invoice & Receipt Audit
    61 invoices (30.5%) and 45 receipts (30%) are flagged VOID. Confirm each void was properly authorised and that no cash flows are concealed within voided records.
  • 5
    Zero-Rate VAT Review
    33 invoices carry a 0% tax rate. Finance should confirm exemption codes are correctly applied and file supporting documentation for audit readiness.
  • 6
    Cash & Internal Transfer Controls
    Cash and Internal Transfer each account for 18.5% of transactions â€” the highest channels. Strengthen dual-authorisation and documentation requirements to reduce fraud and audit exposure.
